Galette Bretonne

Get The French Touch

French Galette Bretonnes are savory buckwheat crepes that are stuffed with a layer of ham, cheese and a fried egg.

Ingredients

  • Whole Wheat Flour
  • Buckheat Flour
  • Egg
  • Salt & Water
  • Butter
  • Ham
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Mix the Flour with the Egg and the Water. Add some Salt and start to mix it up
  2. Heat up a pan if you have then use a crepes-pan. Add some butter in it and put some liquid dough to the hot oily pan. Spred well by turning the pan quikly.
  3. Add a slice of ham onto the Crepes and some Cheese
  4. Now break the egg into the center of the Crepes. Season with Salt & Pepper, close the Crepes and there you go!
